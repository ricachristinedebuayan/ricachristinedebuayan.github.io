"THE SUMMER CAPITAL AND VEGETABLE BASKET OF CEBU"



A bit history...

Dalaguete is a first class municipality located approximately 84 kilometers south of Cebu City. It is bounded on the north by the Municipality of Argao, on the south by the Municipality of Alcoy, on the west by the municipalities of Badian and Alegria and on the east by Bohol Strait.Hailed as the Vegetable Basket of Cebu and its Official Summer Capital (via Cebu Provincial Board Resolution 215-2006 dated January 23, 2006), the town is well-known for its cooler than the usual highlands where about 60 tons of farm fresh vegetables are harvested and delivered on a daily basis to Cebu City and the neighboring towns and provinces.

Known barangays:

  • Obong
  • Mantalongon

Festivals:

Utanon Festival...
The Municipality of Dalaguete in Cebu celebrates its good harvest through dance and music every month of February. The town fiesta is celebrated annually in honor of its patron saint, San Guillermo.(Read more.)

Casay's Kite Festival...
The Barangay Casay of Dalguete celebrates Kite Festival every April at Casay Airstrip featuring the Search for Kite Festival Queen. Colorful and artistic display of kites will beautify the sky to amaze spectators that will take part of the visual feast. The annual event had attracted out-of-town participants every year and it is limited to 25 entries only.(Watch the video below.)
