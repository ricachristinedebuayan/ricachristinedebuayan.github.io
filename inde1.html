King and Queen MAZE CHALLENGE is a game with passages, this are platforms arranged to form a maze, where two players can play the game.The players can only move or run through the space where there’s no platforms/fences/walls. Players work together and their goal is to collect all the GOLDEN MUSHROOM. Once they have collected all the mushroom, they can proceed to another level.There will be different backgrounds for different levels. While the players are moving or running around the maze, there will be enemies that will distract them and as the level increases there will be more enemies coming. This enemies are the posionous mushroom and the bomb which players should avoid. If the enemies hit either of them, they will get killed and the game is over.

" I created this game together with my partner, Annabelle Belcina, as our game project to be submitted for our subject, Guided Study 2, in our second semester. We used PHASER 3 (a fast, free, and fun open source HTML5 game framework that offers WebGL and Canvas rendering across desktop and mobile web browsers) and JavaScript for the development of our game."
